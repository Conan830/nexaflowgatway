Terms & Conditions

Setting up clear Terms and Conditions for a bot or tool is essential to protect yourself, clarify boundaries, and manage user expectations. If your downloader bot operates in an open or educational space, here are the key clauses and terms you should consider including:

1. Disclaimer of Copyright Ownership

  • Clarify that the bot does not host, store, or own any media content on its servers.
  • State that the bot merely acts as a technical utility or interface processing direct user requests.

2. Acceptable Use & User Responsibility

  • Personal Use Only: Specify that the bot is intended solely for personal, non-commercial, or fair-use purposes.
  • Compliance with Local Laws: Mandate that users are strictly responsible for adhering to copyright laws and streaming service Terms of Service in their respective jurisdictions.
  • Prohibited Activities: Expressly forbid automated mass-scraping, commercial resale, redistribution of downloaded content, or public exhibition.

3. Service Availability & Limitations

  • No Guarantee of Uptime: State that the service is provided on an "as-is" and "as-available" basis without any uptime guarantees.
  • Third-Party Changes: Reserve the right to alter, limit, or shut down features if source platforms change their APIs, protocols, or security measures.
  • Usage Limits/Rate Limiting: Disclose any daily download limits or rate-limiting policies implemented to prevent network abuse.

4. Limitation of Liability

  • Disclaim liability for any legal actions, account bans, or damages resulting from user interactions with third-party platforms through your bot.
  • Specify that you are not liable for data loss, server downtime, or external bandwidth costs incurred by the user.

5. Privacy & Data Logging

  • Detail what minimal data is logged (e.g., user IDs, timestamps, requested URLs).
  • Explain if/when logs are deleted and confirm that personal user data is not sold or shared with third parties.

6. DMCA / Takedown Policy

  • Provide a direct contact method (such as an email address) for copyright holders or platform representatives to submit takedown notices or abuse reports.
  • Outline a commitment to investigate and process valid removal requests promptly.

7. No Refunds

  • All transactions are final. No refunds will be provided for any plans under any circumstances.
